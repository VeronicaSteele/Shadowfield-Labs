Labrador Ownership

  • Being a Lab owner is both rewarding and challenging! Here's some basics on what you'll need to have success with this amazing breed.

  • Active Lifestyle

    This is a working breed. Labs have a high energy level, making them most compatible with active adults or families. Proper exercise will make for happier dogs and lessen the chances of poor behavior around the house.

  • Proper nutrition

    A high quality, protein-filled diet will help your dog grow and stay strong and healthy. Be careful not to overfeed - these babies love to eat!

  • Consistent Training

    While labs can be ditzy, they are an intelligent breed. Proper training will stimulate them and reduce poor behavior.
